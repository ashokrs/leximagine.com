Tickster Pet Scan Support

Need help with Tickster Pet Scan? Contact LexImagine support and include your phone model, iOS or Android version, Tickster app version, and a short description of what happened.

Email Support

For account, app, scan-history, snapshot, or beta-testing questions, email:

support@leximagine.com

What To Include

Please include your device model, operating system version, Tickster version, account email, and the steps that led to the issue. Screenshots are helpful when the issue is visual.

Veterinary Care

Tickster is a routine screening and record-keeping aid. It does not diagnose disease or replace veterinary care. If your dog seems unwell, if you find an embedded tick, or if you have medical concerns, contact your veterinarian.
